Develop a Diagram

Write these two questions in your science notebook:

  • Why do the teacher see the music student?
  • Why does the student see themself and not the teacher?

Create a diagram to explain as much as you know about the two questions.

  • Include all the important parts we agreed on and label them.
  • Use pictures, symbols, and words to explain how the parts interact to cause the phenomenon.
  • Record questions that you have if you become stuck.
