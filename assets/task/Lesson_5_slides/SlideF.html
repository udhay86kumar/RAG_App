Test Your Air Parcel

  • Seal your parcel of air (the bottle) with a layer of soap bubble solution.
  • Place it in the hot water and observe what happens.
  • Then place your parcel of air in the cold water and observe what happens.
  • Repeat the process a second time, and record what you observe when you place the bottle in each tub. Use pictures and/or words.
